Thank you for confirming your attendance to TartanHacks 2023! We’re super excited to get hacking with you all, and want to share some information before the event begins on Friday!

Discord

First things first, join our Discord Server! 😎 Through this server, you will be able to interact with other hackers, organizers, and sponsors, and receive important announcements during the hackathon. 

Setting up your team

If you don’t have a team right now, feel free to hop into #matchmaking 😍on Discord to find other people who are also looking for a team! Once your team is ready to go, please create your team and invite your team members on the registration system as soon as you can!

Schedule and prizes

In case you would like to check out our schedule 🗓️ or prize line up in advance, the schedule across both Friday and Saturday, and our prize line up is available now on tartanhacks.com

RSVP No

If you can no longer attend TartanHacks 2023, please login to the registration system and click the “sorry I can’t make it button” 😞Since we’re experiencing overwhelming demand for Tartanhacks this year, letting us know as soon as possible will help us process the waitlist and give hackers the best possible experience.

As always, if you have any questions feel free to email us at tartanhacks@scottylabs.org.

Stay tuned for more updates coming soon! We are so excited to disrupt the weekend with you 🎉
